Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

ActionWatch ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our GitHub App.

2. Data We Collect

When you install ActionWatch, we collect the following data via the GitHub API:

  • Account Information: Your GitHub username and avatar.
  • Repository Data: Names and metadata of repositories where the App is installed.
  • Workflow Data: Names, file paths, and cron schedules of GitHub Actions workflows.
  • Workflow Runs: Status, duration, and timestamps of workflow executions.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use this data solely to:

  • Monitor your scheduled workflows.
  • Detect missed cron jobs.
  • Provide a dashboard of your workflow health.
  • Send alerts (if configured).

4. Data Retention

We retain workflow run history for a limited time to provide historical insights. If you uninstall the App, your data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cronwatch.example.com.
